Low and slow

posted by BGB, November 15, 2016

This week i am extremely excited about a new cooking technique called sous vide. In sous vide cooking, you submerge the food (usually vaccum - sealed in plastic ) into a water bath that is precisely set to the target temperature you want the food to be cooked to. In his book, Cooking for Geeks, Jeff potter describes it as "ultra-low-temperature poaching"
